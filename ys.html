Privacy Policy

ZeLin Hong Kong Company Limited is committed to protecting your personal privacy and data security

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

ZeLin Hong Kong Company Limited (referred to as "we," "the company," or "ZeLin Tech") highly values your privacy rights. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information you provide when using our services.

This policy applies to all services we provide, including but not limited to: travel application development, system integration services, data analytics platforms, international roaming solutions, and Roami eSIM products.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Actively Provide

  • Name, contact phone number, email address
  • Company name, position information
  • Project requirements and business consultation content
  • Account information when using Roami eSIM services

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • Network information (IP address, network type)
  • Usage data (access time, page browsing records)
  • Data collected through cookies and similar technologies

1.3 eSIM Service Specific Information

  • Device identifiers such as IMEI, EID
  • Network usage and traffic data
  • Location information (for network connection optimization)
  • Payment and billing information

2. Purpose of Information Use

2.1 Service Provision

  • Provide travel technology solutions and technical support
  • Process your business inquiries and project requirements
  • Provide network connectivity services for Roami eSIM users
  • Conduct system integration and data analytics services

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze user needs to optimize product features
  • Improve service quality and user experience
  • Develop new technological solutions

2.3 Communication

  • Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests
  • Send service notifications and important updates
  • Provide 24/7 technical support services

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We commit not to sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

3.1 Authorized Sharing

  • With your explicit consent
  • Necessary to provide the services you request

3.2 Legal Requirements

  • Compliance with Hong Kong SAR laws and regulations
  • Cooperation with legitimate government investigations
  • Protection of the company's or users' legitimate rights

3.3 Business Partners

  • Sharing with mobile network operators (eSIM services only)
  • Necessary data processing with technology service providers
  • All partners sign strict confidentiality agreements

4. Data Security Protection

We employ enterprise-level security standards to protect your personal information:

4.1 Technical Protection Measures

  • SSL/TLS encrypted transmission
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Access control management
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

4.2 Administrative Protection Measures

  • Employee data protection training
  • Strict data access permission management
  • Data breach emergency response mechanisms
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery plans

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience:

5.1 Cookie Types

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preference settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve website performance

5.2 Cookie Management

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Our services may include links to or integration with third-party services:

6.1 App Store

Roami eSIM app is distributed through Apple App Store, and Apple's privacy policy applies to app downloads and updates.

6.2 Mobile Network Operators

eSIM services require cooperation with global mobile network operators, and related data processing follows each operator's privacy policy.

6.3 Payment Services

Payment processing is handled by third-party payment service providers, and we do not store your complete payment card information.

7. Data Retention Period

  • Account Information: Deleted within 30 days after account deletion
  • Service Data: Retained for 12 months after service termination
  • Technical Logs: Retained for 6 months for troubleshooting
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by Hong Kong law
  • Legal Requirements: Retained as required by relevant laws and regulations

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right of Access

View the personal information we hold about you

Right of Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate personal information

Right of Erasure

Request deletion of your personal information

Right to Restrict Processing

Restrict our processing of your personal information

Exercising Rights: To exercise the above rights, please contact us through the contact information below. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

9. Cross-Border Data Transfer

Due to our international roaming services, some of your personal information may need to be transferred to regions outside Hong Kong:

  • We assess the data protection level of the destination region before transfer
  • Use contractual terms to ensure data protection standards
  • Only transfer the minimum information necessary to provide services
  • Apply encryption protection to transferred data

10. Protection of Minors

Our services are primarily aimed at business customers and adult users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors under 18. If we discover that we have mistakenly collected information from minors, we will immediately delete the relevant data. If you are a guardian of a minor and discover such circumstances, please contact us immediately.

11. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy based on business development and legal requirements:

  • Major changes will be notified to users 30 days in advance
  • Update notices displayed prominently on the website homepage
  • Change notifications sent via email to registered users
  • Continued use of services indicates agreement to the updated policy
